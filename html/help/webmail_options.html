Options 
 
The Options page simply lists any additional pages that fall under the Options section. These pages include:
  • Account Info - Change your personal information and setup account preferences, such as your name, your signature, how many messages appear per page, and more.
     
  • Mailboxes - Setup one or more of your email accounts on other sites or servers to be checked at the same time using your single EMU Webmail account.
     
  • Filters - Create rules for sorting and filtering your new email, such as filtering out email from specific people, automatically deleting email solicitations, and more.
